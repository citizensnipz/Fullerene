# Session log

**Purpose**: cheap handoff between AI sessions or humans — what happened last, what’s next.

## Instructions

- Append **newest entries at the top** (below the header block).
- Keep entries **short** (bullet lists, links to PRs/commits optional).
- Do not paste secrets or full environment dumps.

## Entry format

```text
## YYYY-MM-DD — [topic] — [owner optional]
- Context:
- Done:
- Verified: (commands run)
- Next:
- Blockers:
```
